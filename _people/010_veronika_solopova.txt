Veronika is working with Natural Language processing methods on the PetraKIP project (Persönliches transparentes KI-basiertes Portfolio für die Lehrerbildung).
Her PhD thesis focuses on the ways linguistic knowledge can be used for explainability and transparency in ethicly sensible fields such as hate speech and propaganda detection and automated essay scoring.
